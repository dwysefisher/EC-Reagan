Former Secretary Of State James A. Baker III To Be Honorary Reagan Fellow At Eureka College

Former Secretary of State James A. Baker III will be named an honorary Ronald Reagan Fellow at Eureka College in a ceremony March 28 at The Union League Club, New York City. A reception will begin at 6 p.m. followed by dinner and the ceremony.
