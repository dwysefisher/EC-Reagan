Reagan's Contribution Changed My Life

Reagan Fellow Alumni Share Their Experience

 

Beginning in 2008, alumni of Eureka College's Ronald W. Reagan Leadership Program have been asked  to submit quotes about the impact of the program on their lives.

The Ronald W. Reagan Leadership Program was established in 1982 by President Ronald Reagan and Eureka College. It is the only such program created by a sitting U.S. President of its kind.

For more information on the program >

 

 

"My participation in the Ronald Reagan Leadership Program included: excellent study programs about leadership retreats during which we played out leadership scenarios, intense discussion about world affairs, the study of the virtues of leadership, and three mentorship experiences. I approached these experiences with an open mind and intellectual curiosity.

The apolitical objectives of the program allowed me to explore leadership as a concept and the many manifestations of leadership. The opportunities, offered via the program, led me to a deep understanding of service leadership and the desire to implement it in my life, career and community. 

I especially note the influence of the program as I critically observe "leaders" in the professional arena and in the wider political and cultural world. My assessment is that the serious and sincere study of leadership is necessary as the global community deals with significant challenges."

Mark H. Brown
Reagan Fellow '87
