Join The Ronald W. Reagan Society of Eureka College

 

At Eureka College, we are “Reagan Forward!” More than any other institution of learning in the world, Reagan’s own alma mater is working hard to share his leadership example to benefit both our students and, through the website, new publications, lectures, and much more, we are working to serve the whole public.  

If you believe the United States of America and the world is served well to have access to the lessons of Ronald Reagan’s leadership, then you are invited to become a member of the Ronald W. Reagan Society with a contribution of $25,000, $10,000, $5,000, $2,500  or a minimum of $1,000.  All contributions are welcome. 
Click here to learn what being a Society Member entails >

 

The Reagan Society has been created to allow people like you to support the values you hold dear through the living legacy of Ronald Reagan at Eureka College. 

 

These are critical times for our future and we need you. Call or email me with your questions.  Thank you for supporting the important work of Eureka College to carry the torch forward.
